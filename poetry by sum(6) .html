Sea Star

Wounded soul seeking comfort in the discomfort entity of havoc.
The known happiness is in waves, the high tides coming at force, it is loud and ambitious.

Everyone talks about it, after all it must be known that it exists from the soul's acknowledgement but as the high becomes the low, silence does it hear.
The discomfort is too much to bear, too much to handle.

Everyone does talk yet only when the destruction aftermath occurs, till then must it be silent?
The comfort of waves is temperament to one wounded soul, must it seek the consciousness of the sea to acquire its knowledge of belief of its chaotic continuing.
The sea continues on, loud, violent, tranquilent, quiet and all embodiment of consciousness.
The stars shine upon as the waves crash.
