HER

Her screams are soundless, reaching a few lengths in
    Hindsight.

Yet the Sirens are on the way, she yearns for death but fears the result of destruction,
    Oh how she is Misunderstood.

Let her dream her
    Violent Pathetic Dreams.

Let it give meaning to her soulless existence in this realm like no other. It is the only thing herself will possess,
The validity of fulfilled destiny is near, intimacy so clear yet so malevolent,
Love or lust, will it bring her to her knees, pleading for mercy,
    Will it heal the hearden cracks?

Think twice to arrive at dawn, the star may shine, screaming for redemption to bring chaos in order.
    Foolish her, but the Clock is Ticking.

Call it love, call it a Whore, her choices are silent by the nature of Law.
Her must leave blindly towards the open black hole of ibis, to her destiny that calls her name.
    Like a siren tying her in, a smirk on her face, havoc inside her head and blood stain hands,
Flaws leaving her mark.

Think once, her destiny in your name, sirens in the distance in silence, the night is finally asleep.
